About Tonguesten

Tonguesten is an early-stage social enterprise that has big ambitions to change the world of language-learning and literacy. We take the world’s best music videos, films and football, and turn them into learning materials. We believe that the learning materials of the future will be different for each user, and will update in real-time. Whether a student likes Arsenal or Lady Gaga, we don’t care - it’s all packed with useful language that can be teased out, using artificial intelligence, to become part of a personalised course.

Our first product ReBeats has been in private beta for three months: it’s Guitar Hero for Learning English.

We’re pretty proud that our users (teachers and students) from 88 countries are playing, on average, for twenty minutes a day.

We’re backed by Telefonica, based at the WAYRA accelerator in Central London, and we’ve just won a prestigious grant to develop a primary school product for the 1 million + kids in the UK who don’t have English at home.

You’ll help us to develop it, while

  • working alongside our NLP team and educational psychologist to map the national English curriculum onto music videos (we’re not joking :) )
  • working with our design and business team to make data-driven decisions
  • getting feedback from kids and teachers who will tell you that you’ve “honestly, honestly come up with the coolest thing ever”
  • seeing the platforms you make undergo rigorous scientific trials in four continents
  • helping to come up with a solution that could change the game for the 1 in 5 people in the UK and EU who cannot read
